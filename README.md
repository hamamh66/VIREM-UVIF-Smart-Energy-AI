# VIREM-UVIF-Smart-Energy-AI

## Overview

This repository presents **VIREM** (Variational Intelligence--Risk--Energy Management), 
a physics-inspired and decision-centric artificial intelligence framework for 
smart energy systems.

The framework is grounded in the **Unified Variational Intelligence Framework (UVIF)** 
and focuses on:

- Uncertainty-aware intelligent energy management
- Explainable and interpretable AI
- Renewable energy forecasting
- Operational stability analysis
- Risk-aware optimization
- Adaptive smart-grid intelligence
- Decision-centric AI architectures

Unlike conventional prediction-centric AI approaches, VIREM integrates prediction, 
uncertainty, operational risk, explainability, and stability-aware optimization 
within a unified variational framework.
